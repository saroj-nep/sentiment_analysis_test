Hello there! Welcome to PARCELCHECK, and thank you for your participation in the study involving our chatbot 'ParcelCheck'. The study is divided into two parts:

1) Conversation with the chatbot (~10 minutes)
2) Post-chat survey (~10 minutes)

First of all, you will need to complete some tasks with the help from the chatbot.After, the chatbot will provide you the link to the survey. Your tasks:

Task 1: Suppose you are one of our customers and have been waiting for a parcel. Your parcel's tracking number is 123456. The parcel has not arrived to you yet, and you are curious about the status of the delivery. Find out about the status of your parcel by conversing with the chatbot.

Task 2: You need to send a parcel to a foreign country. You do not know about the cost, procedures and time taken for sending the parcel. Find these information out by conversing with the chatbot.

Task 3:Think of a hypothetical issue relevant to parcel deilvery service. Try to solve the issue with the help of the chatbot.

Important! Based on your interaction with the chatbot, your conversation might end before you have completed all the tasks. In such case, simply follow the link to the survey to complete the 2nd part of the survey. For convenience, the task descriptions can be toggled on the right side of the chatbot.
